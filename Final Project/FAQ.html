What was the reason for creating this website?

This website was created to give students who don't have the money to buy regular price laptops a chance to find a cheap laptop that they can use for whatever they need it for.

What kind of laptops can I find here?

Here at Cheap Laptops for students, you can laptops of many different brands and kinds, such as regular pc laptops, macbooks, and netbooks.

Why am I only allowed to make two purchases at a time?

Our inventory is still pretty limited, as this is a newly created website and we are still lacking the funds to deal with a large amount of orders. We hope to have more stock and a bigger variety of brands in the future as we grow.

Will ther be other products besides laptops sold in the future?

Yes. In the future, we hope to expand our inventory to include more products such as cellphones, tablets, and other electronics.
